County Income Levels vs. School Ratings in the Southeastern US

Is there a strong coorelation between school rankings and income per capita in Southeast states at county level?

Our hypothesis was that there must be a strong coorelation between school success level and income level of states. However, when we calculate coorelation coefficient between two metrics, r-value is clculated as 0.11, which shows a very low level of coorelation.
